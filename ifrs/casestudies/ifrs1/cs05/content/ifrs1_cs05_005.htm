Subsidiaries previously not consolidated under local GAAP
Background The outcome

Atkins Co. acquired a building in 1990 at a cost of €1 million. Under local GAAP, it did not depreciate the building. Tetris Co. subsequently acquired Atkins on 1 January 2000. Under local GAAP, Tetris did not consolidate Atkins.

Tetris Co. adopts IFRS for its 31 December 2007 annual report with 1 January 2006 as its date of transition. As a consequence of adopting IFRS, Tetris must consolidate Atkins and consider whether it should depreciate the building. How do you think they measured the building in Tetris Co’s consolidated IFRS accounts?

Since the asset is part of an unconsolidated subsidiary, it is subject to the requirements of IFRS 1 relating to Business Combinations (contained in Appendix C). Full retrospective application of IFRS is required back to the date the building was acquired by Tetris - the date Atkins was acquired - January 2000.
