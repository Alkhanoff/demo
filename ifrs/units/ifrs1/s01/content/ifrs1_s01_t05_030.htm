What you’ll learn
The four scenarios in this module will help you research the key aspects of IFRS 1.

Having worked through these scenarios, you’ll have an understanding of:

  • General principles of IFRS 1 (Scenario 1)
  • Voluntary exemptions that an entity may elect to use (Scenario 2)
  • Mandatory exceptions (Scenario 3)
  • Presentation and disclosure requirements (Scenario 4)
