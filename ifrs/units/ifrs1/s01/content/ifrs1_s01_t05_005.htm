An important message

Please note that, although this is the first IFRS (IFRS 1), it is not the best standard to start with in your understanding IFRS.This is because the module assumes a minimum level of knowledge about other standards, including:

  • Deemed cost
  • Employee benefits (IAS 19)
  • Cumulative translation differences (IAS 21)
  • Business combinations (IFRS 3)
  • Compound financial instruments (IAS 32)
  • Interim financial reporting (IAS 34)
  • Impairment of assets (IAS 36)
  • Financial Instruments: Recognition and Measurement (IAS 39)
