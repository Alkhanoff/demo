Introduction

Congratulations. You have now completed IFRS 1:First-time Adoption of International Financial Reporting Standards and are ready to take the assessment.

There are ten questions that can cover material from any area of the IFRS.

You need to score at least 70% to pass. If you pass the test, you will be able to print a certificate that documents your successful completion of the unit and your score.

If you don’t pass the test you will be given your mark and a list of Coach me sessions that cover the areas you didn’t correctly answer questions on. Working through these sessions should enable you to successfully pass the assessment.

Good luck!
