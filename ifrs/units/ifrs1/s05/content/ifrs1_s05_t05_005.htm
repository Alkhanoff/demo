Preparation
As the recently appointed financial controller of Clover Manufacturing, you’ve been called upon to describe the transition to IFRS. You need to explain the general principles, rules, definitions and scope of IFRS 1.

It’s early Monday morning and the aroma of coffee drifts into your office as you make preparations to give a general presentation to the company’s CEO, CFO and COO. This meeting was requested in order to determine how and when Clover should make the transition to IFRS.

Checking your voice mail, you pick up a message that must have arrived over the weekend. It’s from your boss Garrett, the CFO. Hopefully, the message will contain information to help you prepare for this important question and answer session.

But first things first: time for a cup of freshly brewed coffee.
