Subsidiary as a first-time adopter later than its parent
If a subsidiary (or associate or joint venture) becomes a first-time adopter later than its parent, the subsidiary shall, in its separate financial statements, measure its assets and liabilities in one of two ways:
  • Carrying amounts included in parent’s consolidated financial statements
  • Carrying amounts determined by applying IFRS 1 at the subsidiary’s date of transition to IFRS
