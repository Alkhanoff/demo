Summary
In this Coach me session, you have learnt the following about business combinations exemptions:
  • Not required to restate prior business combinations accounted for under a standard different from IFRS applicable at the date of reporting.
  • Still need to eliminate assets and liabilities that do not meet the recognition criteria under IFRS outside of a business combination (adjustment to retained earnings except in case of intangible assets then corresponding adjustment to goodwill).
  • Test goodwill in opening IFRS statement of financial position for impairment.
