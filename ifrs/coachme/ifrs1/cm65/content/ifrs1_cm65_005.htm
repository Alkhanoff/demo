Introduction
This Coach me section explains the presentation and disclosure requirements for a first-time adopter.

IFRS 1 does not provide exemptions from the presentation and disclosure requirements in other IFRS.

In this Coach me section, you will learn about:

  • how many years of comparative information have to be included
  • the information an entity has to provide in order to explain how the transition from previous GAAP to IFRS affected its reported financial position, performance and cash flows (reconciliation of equity, reconciliation of profit or loss, additional disclosures regarding impairment of assets)
  • how to reflect the correction of errors made under previous GAAP
  • interim financial reports
